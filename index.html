Section 2

Types of Energy

Different situations show different forms of energy. A single object can even have more than one type at the same time.

Check Your Understanding

A backpack sitting on a desk is not moving. Does it have kinetic energy, potential energy, or both?

It has potential energy because it is stored in a raised position, but it does not have kinetic energy unless it starts moving.

Section 3

Energy Transfer vs Energy Transformation

These ideas sound similar, but they are not the same. One is energy moving between objects, and the other is energy changing form.

Energy moving from one object to another. The form can stay the same while the energy changes location.

  • A hot cup transfers thermal energy to your hands.
  • A moving pool ball transfers kinetic energy to another ball.
  • Sunlight transfers energy to the ground and warms it.

Energy changing from one form to another. The energy is still there, but it looks different afterward.

  • A lamp changes electrical energy into light and thermal energy.
  • Your body changes chemical energy from food into motion.
  • A speaker changes electrical energy into sound energy.

One event can include both ideas.

A hair dryer shows both. Inside the dryer, electrical energy transforms into thermal energy and kinetic energy of moving air. Then that warm air transfers thermal energy to wet hair.

Check Your Understanding

When a battery-powered toy car starts moving, is that mostly an energy transfer or an energy transformation?

It is mostly an energy transformation because chemical energy in the battery changes into electrical energy and then kinetic energy.

Section 4

Conservation of Energy

means energy cannot be created or destroyed. It can only move or change form.

Roller Coaster Example

At the top of the hill, the coaster has lots of potential energy. As it drops, that stored energy becomes kinetic energy. Some energy also becomes sound and thermal energy because of friction.

Food Example

The chemical energy in food does not vanish. Your body transforms it into motion, body heat, and even sound when your shoes hit the floor.

Electricity Example

When a phone charger is plugged in, electrical energy can become stored chemical energy in the battery, plus some thermal energy.

Energy Story Map

Stored > Moves > Changes Form > Total Stays the Same

This is why scientists say energy is conserved. It may spread out or become harder to notice, but it does not disappear.

Check Your Understanding

If a moving skateboard slows down, where did the “missing” energy go?

It was not destroyed. Some of the energy changed into thermal energy from friction and sound energy from the wheels and board.
